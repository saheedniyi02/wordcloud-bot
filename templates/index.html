@Wordcloud bot is a twitter bot which generates word cloud image of words that are commonly used in same statements or conversations with a requested image. The word cloud image is generated from Nigerian based data, gotten from “Nairaland.com”- Africa’s largest discussion forum. Posts (without comments/replies) by users on Nairaland will be used to generate the word cloud images.
